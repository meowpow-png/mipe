# Potion Naming

Use names that sound as though they belong in the age of wizards and alchemists.

Do **not** accept or generate modern, scientific, generic, or humorous names such as:

* Health Potion
* Mana Potion
* Speed Boost
* Magic Juice
* Healing Mix

If a name does not fit this style, reject it and propose suitable alternatives.

Favor names using:

* elixir
* philter
* draught
* essence
* tincture
* mythical creatures
* celestial bodies
* arcane forces
* legendary materials

Examples:

* Elixir of Emberblood
* Draught of Moonveil
* Philter of Verdant Grace
* Essence of the Sapphire Wyrm
* Tincture of Whispering Ash
